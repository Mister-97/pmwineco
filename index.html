Our Story
"Wine was never just for one kind of person. It was always for everyone — we're just here to prove it."

PM Wine Lounge is a Black-owned and operated wine business with a mission as simple as it is powerful: introduce the versatile world of wines to a new generation of enthusiasts.

With a focus on young adults, we curate a diverse array of wines from different regions and producers — inviting our audience to explore, expand their palates, and find bottles they truly love. Not because they're supposed to, but because they want to.

We're especially passionate about clean, minimal-intervention wines — bottles that let the land speak, with nothing hidden and nothing added. That's what we drink. That's what we sell. And that's what we believe wine should be.

Chicago, IL — Est. 2024
Why PM Wine

The PM Difference

01
Global Curation

We source from 12+ wine regions — from Mendoza to Provence, Nahe to Napa. Every bottle is hand-picked by us, not an algorithm.

02
Clean & Honest

Minimal intervention wines are at our core. No excessive additives, no shortcuts — just clean, honest winemaking the way it was meant to be.

03
Education First

Every bottle we carry comes with context. We want you to know what's in your glass — where it's from, how it was made, and why it matters.
